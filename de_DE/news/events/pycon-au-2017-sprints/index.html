PyCon AU 2017 Sprints

Montag, august 7, 2017

BeeWare will be running a sprint at PyCon AU 2017 Sprints.

Russell Keith-Magee and Katie McLaughlin and Amber Brown will be at the sprints, and will be more than willing to help First Time Contributors earn their own shiny Challenge Coin .

For more information, check the PyCon AU 2017 Sprints website

What is a Sprint?

Code sprints are events held normally after a conference where attendees can use the conference venue to work in small groups on various projects. Learn more about how sprints work with the PyCon US 'What is a sprint?' video
